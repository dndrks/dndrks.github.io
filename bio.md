### words

[dani derks](https://dndrks.com) is part of the day-to-day operations at [Whimsical Raps](https://www.whimsicalraps.com) and a member of the extended universe of [monome](https://monome.org). Their professional work involves assisting with the development of new musical instruments, testing and iterating interface designs, and producing accessible technical documentation for artists. Their personal projects are centered on coding through improvisation, creating their own environments for playful and exploratory music performance gestures.
